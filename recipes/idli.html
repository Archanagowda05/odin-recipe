Idli-Chutney

the best south indian breakfast

Description
idli-chutney is the popular breakfast in south india consisting of soft , fluffy and steamed rice cakes served along with the flavourful coconut chutney

Ingredients
  • rice
  • urad dal
  • coconut
  • chillies
  • salt
steps
  1. soak rice and urad dal for about 4-6 hours
  2. grind the batter and let it ferment for 7-8 hours
  3. after fermentation cook it on steam
