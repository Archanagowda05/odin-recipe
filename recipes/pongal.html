pongal

the best south indian breakfast

Description
pongal-chutney is the popular breakfast in south india served along with the flavourful coconut chutney

Ingredients
  • rice
  • dal
  • coconut
  • chillies
  • salt
steps
  1. cook rice and dal with salt and turmeric
  2. add mustard, jeera and chillies in hot oil
  3. mix both and serve hot
